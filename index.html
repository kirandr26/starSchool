AboutStar School

Star school was inaugurated on 6th April, 2011, by Mr. C. Vijayakumar, the founder of Muthusamy Memorial Educational Trust and got affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. Affiliation No: 1930321. The school aims at the encouragement of self-discipline, high intellectual achievements and physical fitness, through a holistic approach.

The Central Board of Secondary Education's prime focus, “Innovation in teaching, learning methodologies by devising student-friendly and student-centred paradigm” is also the main focus of Star School Montessori & CBSE.
