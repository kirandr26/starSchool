Mission
  • Educate all those who enter its portals making them lifelong learners
  • Engage the very best tutors to implement proven national and international curricula
  • Teach through contemporary but appropriate methodologies and technologies
  • Develop qualities of tolerance, humility and compassion in an environment provided with Indian ethos
  • Foster attributes of inquiry, critical thinking, creativity and leadership
  • Nurture inherent talents and interests of its students and make them dream the dreams that will shape their lives as caring, responsible, global citizens.

AboutStar School

  • Star school was inaugurated on 6th April, 2011, by Mr. C. Vijayakumar, the founder of Muthusamy Memorial Educational Trust and got affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. Affiliation No: 1930321. The school aims at encouragement of self-discipline, high intellectual achievements and physical fitness, through a holistic approach.
  • The Central Board of Secondary Education's prime focus, “Innovation in teaching, learning methodologies by devising student-friendly and student-centred paradigm” is also the main focus of Star School.
  • Star School takes a meritorious walk under the guidance of Prof.R.Pandiaraj, M.A., P.G.D.T.E., Administrative Officer and Mr.G.Sivaraman, Advisor.
  • The qualified, dedicated and enthusiastic staff team of Star stretches its hands to shape children with the coat of multifarious activities in the school.
  • Individual guidance is given to all the deeds of children. The integration of exponential learning and audio-visual learning is done for every concept of every class to achieve the mission of Star school, “Man-making Education”.
  • Our curriculum is interwoven with six basic themes as the following: “Who we are, where we are in place and time, how we express ourselves, how the world works, how we organize ourselves and share the planet”. Towards lubricating these areas, Star school caters for children with extra-curricular and co-curricular activities like Dance, Music, Club activities, Art, Dramatics, Yoga, Karate, Chess, Skating, Carrom and outdoor games.
  • Other essential facilities like lunch, transport to all areas of Karur, RO water, spacious playground, Computer Lab, Composite Science Lab, Maths Lab, an aseptic and spacious dining hall, a digital Library, Exclusive Montessori environments, unstained Washrooms etc. add quality to our school.
